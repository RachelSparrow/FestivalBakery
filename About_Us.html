Our Store

Our Goal

The Festival Foods Bakery is dedicated to providing high-quality products for our customers to enjoy. Our aim is to offer a vast variety of food and a growing selection of items for our customers to choose from. Our selection includes anything from donuts to breads to desserts; we also have a section of Gluten Free products for our customers who need a certified product! We also have a small selection of Dairy Free and Sugar Free foods!

History

Festival Foods Bakery was first known as McGlynn's Bakery over 20 years ago. But in 2001, the McGlynn's no longer serviced retail bakeries and Festival Foods had to find a new supplier to provide fresh baked product. It was then that the owners found Taste of Scandinavia Bakeries and decided to use them as a supplier. As of today, Festival Foods bakeries are still using Taste of Scandinavia to provide fresh baked donuts, cakes, breads, and more!
